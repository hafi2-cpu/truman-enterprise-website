Help us keep the Enterprise sailing for generations to come

Help Needed!

Please help our small charity to buy a new, accessible, hybrid narrowboat so our fabulous team of volunteers can continue providing day trips for people who are elderly, disabled, unwell or disadvantaged on the South Staffs canals.

We are looking to replace our current boat, Enterprise, after 50 years of brilliant service to hundreds of community groups and families. A new boat will give our passengers and volunteers more modern, comfortable and convenient facilities — and our trips can continue for many years to come.

Raised so far: £67,000 Target: £100,000

£33,000 still needed — please help us reach our goal
