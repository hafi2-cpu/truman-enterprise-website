Get Involved

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and make a real difference

Volunteering

Be Part of Something Special

Our trust is dedicated to providing memorable canal boat experiences for elderly and disabled members of our community. Every trip we offer brings joy, connection, and a sense of freedom to those who might otherwise miss out. We can only continue this vital work with your support.

⚓

Volunteer as a Crew Member

Love being on the water? Like meeting new people? Under the supervision of an experienced skipper, our crew members are essential to every trip. Help with lock operations, interact with passengers, and ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience. No previous experience necessary — just enthusiasm and a caring nature. Full training provided.

🚢

Train to be a Skipper

Experienced boat crew can volunteer to be trained as a skipper — the person in charge of each trip. Good boat handling skills needed and full training provided by our NCBA qualified trainer. You'll gain valuable skills while making a real difference to people's lives.

📋

Administrative Support

Behind every boat trip is careful planning and coordination. We need volunteers to help with booking systems, maintaining passenger records, scheduling trips, coordinating volunteers, and general office administration.

📣

Marketing & Communications

Help us spread the word! We're looking for volunteers to manage social media, create promotional materials, write newsletters, take photos during trips, maintain our website, and help with community outreach. Your skills could help us reach more people who would benefit from our services.

🎪

Fundraising & Events

Support our financial sustainability by helping organise fundraising events, writing grant applications, managing donor relationships, or coordinating charity activities. Every pound raised keeps our boat running and our community thriving.

🔧

Maintenance & Technical

Do you have practical skills? We need help with boat maintenance, equipment checks (especially cleaning and painting), ensuring our disabled lift operates safely, general repairs, and keeping our marina facilities in good condition. Your expertise keeps us safe and operational.

🏛️

Trustee & Governance

Join our board of trustees and help shape the future of our organisation. We welcome people with skills in finance, legal matters, charity governance, or strategic planning. Usually requires attendance at quarterly meetings.

💛

Donating Your Time

Whatever your skills, interests or available time, there's a way you can help. Whether you can spare a few hours a month or want to make a regular commitment, every contribution matters. You'll join a friendly team of dedicated volunteers who share a passion for making our waterways accessible to all.
